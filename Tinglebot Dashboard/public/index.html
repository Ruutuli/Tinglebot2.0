How It Works

  • All suggestions are submitted anonymously
  • You must be logged in with Discord to submit
  • You must be a member of the Discord server
  • We review every suggestion carefully
  • Popular ideas may be implemented in future updates
  • You can submit multiple suggestions
  • Perfect for server improvements, new features, or reporting issues

Tips for Great Suggestions

  • Be specific and detailed about your idea
  • Explain the problem you're solving or improvement you want
  • Consider how it benefits the server community
  • No links or URLs are allowed in suggestions
  • All suggestions remain completely anonymous

Member Lore Submission

Fill this out to submit your own lore for the Roots of the Wild world! Please keep these things in mind:

1. Please have your lore concise and to the point for ease of reading and adding to the website.
2. Submit lore that is open enough for other members to not use if they choose not to.
3. Alternatively, submit lore that you'd be willing to have others build upon and/or mods have to change a bit.
4. Mods may deny lore that veers too heavily into something we'd want to review on a case by case basis.

Login with Discord to submit your lore and help expand the Roots of the Wild universe.

Brief answers here please, just for people perusing the site to quickly see topics of interest.

How It Works

  • All lore submissions are reviewed by moderators
  • You must be logged in with Discord to submit
  • You must be a member of the Discord server
  • Lore should be concise and well-written
  • Keep lore open for others to use or build upon
  • Mods may request changes or deny inappropriate content
  • Help expand the Roots of the Wild universe

Tips for Great Lore

  • Keep it concise - 3 paragraphs maximum
  • Make it open for others to use or ignore
  • Be willing to let others build upon your ideas
  • Focus on world-building and atmosphere
  • Avoid overly specific or restrictive lore
  • Consider how it fits with existing lore
  • Write in a style that matches the world's tone
