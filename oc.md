## Overview

All character creation, editing, and submission is done through the Tinglebot website at **https://tinglebot.xyz**. This guide will walk you through the entire process from creating your character to getting it approved.

**What You Need:**
- A Discord account
- Access to the Tinglebot website
- Your character information ready (name, stats, etc.)

---

## Getting Started

### Step 1: Log In
1. Go to **https://tinglebot.xyz**
2. Log in with your Discord account (if not already logged in)

### Step 2: Create a New Character
1. In the navigation menu at the top, click **"Characters"** (dropdown menu)
2. Click **"Create Character"**
3. You'll see the character creation form
4. Fill out all required fields (see below)

---

## Creating Your Character

### Required Fields

**You must fill out ALL of these before you can submit:**

- **Name** - Your character's name (must be unique, cannot be changed later)
- **Icon** - Upload a picture of your character (required)
- **Age** - Enter a number (e.g., 18, 27)
- **Height** - Enter in centimeters (e.g., 160, 172.5) - it will convert to feet/inches automatically
- **Pronouns** - Choose: she/her, he/him, they/them, or enter custom
- **Race** - Start typing and pick from the list (Hylian, Zora, Goron, etc.)
- **Home Village** - Pick one: Inariko, Rudania, or Vhintl
- **Job** - Pick from the dropdown menu
- **Max Hearts** - All characters start with 3
- **Max Stamina** - All characters start with 5
- **Attack** - Enter your attack stat
- **Defense** - Enter your defense stat

### Optional Fields (Recommended)

- **Application Art** - Upload artwork of your character (highly recommended!)
- **Personality** - Describe your character's personality
- **History** - Write your character's backstory
- **Extras** - Any additional information
- **Starting Gear** - Weapon, Shield, Chest Armor, Leg Armor (optional)

### Saving Your Work

- Click **"Create and Save Character"** to save your character
- Your character will be saved as a **Draft**
- You can edit drafts as many times as you want
- Come back later to finish filling it out - your work is saved!

## Submitting for Review

### When You're Ready to Submit

1. **Make sure everything is filled out:**
   - All required fields must have something in them
   - Double-check your information is correct

2. **Go to your character's page:**
   - After creating, you'll be taken there automatically
   - Or find it from your Profile page
   - URL: `https://tinglebot.xyz/ocs/[your-character-name]`

3. **Click the "Submit for Review" button**
   - This button appears at the top of your character page
   - Only shows when your character is saved as a Draft

4. **Confirm you want to submit**
   - Click "Submit" in the confirmation dialog
   - Your character status changes to **"Pending Review"**
   - You'll see a success message

### What Happens Next

- Your application is posted to Discord for moderator review
- Moderators will review your character
- You'll receive notifications about the review progress via Discord DMs and the dashboard

## Understanding Application Status

Your character can be in one of four statuses:

### üü¶ Draft
- **What it means:** Your character is saved but not submitted
- **What you can do:**
  - Edit all fields
  - Save changes
  - Submit for review
- **What you see:** "Save" or "Save Changes" button, "Submit for Review" button

### üüß Pending Review
- **What it means:** Your character is submitted and waiting for moderator votes
- **What you can do:**
  - View your character (read-only)
  - Wait for moderator review
- **What you see:** Status badge showing "Pending Review" with vote counts
- **Vote requirement:** Needs **4 approve votes** from moderators

### üü• Needs Changes
- **What it means:** A moderator requested changes to your character
- **What you can do:**
  - Edit your character to fix the issues
  - Read moderator feedback
  - Resubmit after making changes
- **What you see:** "Edit Character" button, "Resubmit" button, feedback section
- **Note:** Only **1 needs_changes vote** is required to trigger this status

### üü© Approved
- **What it means:** Your character has been approved and is now active!
- **What you can do:**
  - View your character
  - Edit certain fields (limited editing)
  - Use your character in the game
- **What you see:** Status badge showing "Approved"

## What Happens During Review

### The Review Process

1. **Submission**
   - Your character is posted to Discord for moderator review
   - Moderators are notified

2. **Moderator Voting**
   - Moderators review your character on the **Character Moderation** page
   - They can vote:
     - ‚úÖ **Approve** - Character looks good
     - ‚ö†Ô∏è **Needs Changes** - Issues need to be fixed

3. **Vote Requirements**
   - **Approval:** Requires **4 approve votes**
   - **Needs Changes:** Requires only **1 needs_changes vote** (immediate)

4. **Notifications**
   - You'll receive Discord DMs when:
     - Your character is approved
     - Your character needs changes (with feedback)
   - You'll also see notifications on the dashboard

### Where to Check Status

- **Your Character Page** - Shows your current status, vote counts, and feedback
- **Dashboard Notifications** - All updates about your application

## If Changes Are Needed

### When You Receive "Needs Changes"

1. **Check the Feedback**
   - Go to your character page
   - Look for the feedback section
   - Read what the moderator said needs to be fixed

2. **Edit Your Character**
   - Click **"Edit Character"** button
   - Make the necessary changes
   - Click **"Save Changes"**

3. **Resubmit**
   - After saving, click **"Resubmit"** button
   - Confirm the resubmission
   - Your character version will increment (v1 ‚Üí v2, etc.)

### Important Notes About Resubmission

- When you resubmit, all previous votes are cleared (fresh start)
- Your application version number increases (v1 ‚Üí v2 ‚Üí v3, etc.)
- The original Discord post is updated with your new information
- A notification is posted saying your application has been updated
- Moderators will review your updated version

### Making Multiple Resubmissions

- You can resubmit as many times as needed until approved
- Each resubmission creates a new version number
- Previous votes don't carry over - it's a fresh review each time

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**"I can't find my character"**
- Go to your Profile page on the website
- Make sure you're logged in with the correct Discord account

**"Submit button doesn't appear"**
- Make sure you've saved your character first (click "Create and Save Character")
- Check that ALL required fields are filled out
- Try refreshing the page

**"I can't edit my character"**
- **Draft:** You can edit everything
- **Pending Review:** Can't edit (waiting for moderators)
- **Needs Changes:** You can edit everything
- **Approved:** Limited editing (some fields are locked)

**"I didn't get a notification"**
- Check your Discord DMs (make sure server members can DM you)
- Check the dashboard: https://tinglebot.xyz/notifications
- Check the Character Creation Channel on Discord

**"I need to make changes but don't see feedback"**
- Go to your character page
- Look for the feedback section
- Check your Discord DMs for the notification
- Check the notifications page on the dashboard

**"I can't resubmit"**
- Make sure your character status says "Needs Changes"
- Make sure you've saved your edits first
- Try refreshing the page

### Still Need Help?

1. Check the notifications page on the dashboard
2. Check your Discord DMs
3. Ask in the Discord server
4. Contact a moderator
